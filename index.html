Math1

HS 1st

This subject covers basic concepts in mathematics and algebra. Topics include linear equations, quadratic equations, functions and graphs, exponents, and logarithms.

Math2

HS 1st

This subject focuses on geometric concepts and set theory. Topics include trigonometric functions, trigonometric ratios, and the concept of sets. Students learn how to solve geometric problems.

Calculus

HS 2nd

This field deals with changing quantities. Students learn about limits, differentiation, and integration. Calculus plays a crucial role in natural sciences and engineering.

Statistics

HS 2nd

Probability and Statistics. This subject involves collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data. Students learn how to deal with uncertainty and perform statistical inference and prediction.

Geometry

HS 3rd

Geometry and Vectors. This subject focuses on understanding objects and relationships in space. Topics include the concept of vectors and solving geometric problems.

SAT

HS 3rd

This subject is aimed at preparing for the SAT exam, covering a comprehensive range of topics including Mathematics 1, Mathematics 2, Calculus, Probability and Statistics, Geometry and Vectors. The SAT exam is a national exam for university admissions, so this subject is highly important.
